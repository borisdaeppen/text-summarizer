How do I delete a rotator, rotator URL, or rotator group?

If you want to delete a rotator, just click on the Edit menu to the right of any rotator and select Delete Rotator from the drop down menu:





To delete a rotator URL, edit the specific URL you want t delete and you’ll find a Delete URL button at the very bottom of the rotator URL settings:





To delete a rotator group—which does NOT delete the rotators themselves—go to the Tools menu at the top of every page and select the Group Manager. From there, choose the Delete Groups option. Choose the rotator group you want to delete, then click the Delete Group button:



If you want to delete the group and all of the rotators within it, the easiest way is to first select the group on the Rotators page to show only the rotators in the group, then delete each rotator, which will also delete all the rotator URLs associated with that rotator. Finally delete the group itself.
