Why do I get a 404 or 410 error when clicking my link?

ClickMagick doesn’t generate 404 or 410 errors—ever—so if you’re getting one of these errors, either you have a typo in one of the URLs that you’ve entered or there’s a problem with one of the websites you’re promoting.
