How do I cloak or uncloak an existing custom domain?

If you’ve already created a custom domain in the Domain Manager, and you want to change its current cloak setting, you can easily cloak or uncloak the domain by selecting the setting you want in the drop-down menu to the right of custom domain’s name:





Your choices are “Cloaked” or “Redirect”. “Redirect” means that the domain will not be cloaked (or masked), but will simply redirect to your tracking or rotator link.
