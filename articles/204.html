How do I delete a link or a link group?

If you want to delete a tracking link, it’s really simple. Just click on the Edit menu to the right of any link and choose Delete Link from the drop down menu:



 

To delete a Link Group—which does NOT delete the links themselves—go to the Tools menu at the top of every page and select the Group Manager. From there, choose the Delete Groups option. Choose the link group you want to delete, then click the Delete Group button:




If you want to delete the group and all of the links within it, the easiest way is to first select the group on the Links page to show only the links in the group, then delete each link. Finally delete the group itself.
