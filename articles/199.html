How do I setup MOBE with ClickMagick?

MOBE sells training products for small businesses which you can sell as an affiliate.

To track your sales as an affiliate, you’ll use Postback URL tracking. You’ll pass a unique click ID from ClickMagick to MOBE and their system will pass that back to us when a sale is made, which will attribute the sale to the tracking link in your account.

Here’s how to set up tracking with MOBE:
 
1.    Set up a tracking link in your ClickMagick account using your affiliate URL from MOBE as the Primary URL, with the [clickid] token added at the end to pass a unique sub-ID to MOBE

For example, if your affiliate URL is this:

https://rookieprofitsystem.com/rps-video/?aff_id=xxxxxx

Then the URL you would use as the Primary URL of your tracking link would be:

https://rookieprofitsystem.com/rps-video/?aff_id=xxxxxx&ho_sub=[clickid]

MOBE uses ho_sub as a query string variable to let you send data to it.
 
2.    In your MOBE dashboard, you’ll need to add a Postback URL. That URL would be:

http://www.clkmg.com/api/s/post/?uid=$userid&s1={ho_sub}&amt={payout}&ref=
 

Your MOBE sales will now show up in your ClickMagick account.
