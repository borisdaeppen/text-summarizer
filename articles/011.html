How come I don’t see the pop up on my links?

Remember, your “cookie duration” setting determines how often users will see a pop up—including yourself.

If you’re testing a link with a pop up and you don’t see it, that means whatever cookie duration you’ve set hasn’t passed since you last saw that pop up.

Wait a bit, and then you’ll see it again.
