Is it okay to use ClickMagick with Network Marketing?

You bet. We love helping network marketers build their businesses using online marketing techniques.

If using online methods to build your network marketing business is new to you, you’ll want to learn some basic concepts like “list-building” so you can develop a list of people to talk with on an ongoing basis.

The key there is to provide valuable content. If you just try to sell them into your opportunity with every email you send, your subscribers won’t be subscribers for very long…
