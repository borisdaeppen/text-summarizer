How do I track conversions with ClickMagick?

ClickMagick supports the 3 “industry standard” ways to track conversions – Pixels, Postback URLs and Sub-IDs.


Tracking Pixels – The easiest way to track conversions.

This is what you’ll want to use if you’re promoting your own stuff, and/or if your affiliate network allows you to add tracking pixels for the offers you’re promoting.

Just add our Action tracking pixel (for tracking opt-ins, webinar registrations, etc.) or our Sales tracking pixel to the “thank you” page that users see after taking the action you want to track, and that’s all there is to it!

Postback URLs – Some of the larger affiliate networks support Postback URLs which is another way you can track your affiliate conversions.
