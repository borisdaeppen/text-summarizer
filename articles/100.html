How can I publicly share my stats for a link?

Sharing your stats for an individual tracking link or rotator URL with your clients, partners, friends, or just anyone in the general public is easy to do …

For a tracking link, just select “Public Stats” from the drop-down menu to the far right of any link.

For a rotator URL, simply find the URL in your rotator and click the “Share” link in the Stats column.

Either of these will result in a “public stats page” that you can easily share with anyone by simply giving them the URL to the resulting page.
 
 
Tip: In the “Public Stats” Settings of your User Profile you can also customize your public stats pages by adding a custom logo and by optionally displaying a ClickMagick banner that redirects users to your affiliate link.
