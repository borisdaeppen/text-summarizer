So what can I do with these MagickBars?

Pretty much whatever you want!

As with pop ups, the sky is the limit and you have full control over the content of your MagickBars.

It really depends on what you’re trying to do, but MagickBars are great for things like ...
  • Offering a bonus on an affiliate page
  • Highlighting something on your own site
  • Prominently displaying links on any page
  • Grabbing the users attention with audio
  • Creating urgency with a countdown timer
  • Building your list with an opt-in form
Unlike pop ups, MagickBars stick around unless the user closes them (if you allow them to) …

… so they’re perfect for highlighting or reminding users about something, or for showing any other message or content you want to keep in front of them.
