How do I completely hide my referers?

If you want to hide or “blank” your referers so the sites you’re sending traffic to can’t tell where it’s coming from, it’s super easy to do …

Just go into the Advanced Settings for any link or rotator and check the box that says “Blank Referrers” near the bottom, save your changes, and that’s all there is it it.

Important: This requires an extra redirect which technically increases the time it takes for the user to see the page you’re sending them to.

Please do NOT enable this unless you understand the purpose of it, and you have a good reason for using it.
