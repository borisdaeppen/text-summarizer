Can I track email follow-ups for my funnel too?

You sure can!

If you’re using ClickMagick’s advanced “Entry Link” setting of your tracking link, you can easily track the email follow-ups to your funnel.

Just create a tracking link for each email in your follow-up sequence, and be sure to add it to the primary link group for the funnel you want to associate the emails with.

The only other thing you have to do is make sure you include the word “email” somewhere in the “friendly” link name for any email tracking links …





... and that will allow the system to track conversions from your follow-ups as part of your overall funnel.

To see how this is done, watch this video which will show you exactly how to set up your email tracking links:
 
How to Track Email Follow-up Messages
 
Caution: You can NOT add a ClickMagick tracking pixel to the Pixel/Code section of an email tracking link set up this way – it’s just incompatible with the way email tracking links work when use with the Entry Link setting.
