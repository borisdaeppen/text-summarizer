What is the Pixel Debugger used for?

The Pixel Debugger is found in the Tools menu under the Tracking Pixels sub-menu and you can use it to test and confirm the proper integration of ClickMagick tracking pixels, as well as view pixels that have “fired” and see what tracking links the conversions were attributed to.

If you want to know if you correctly installed a tracking pixel on a certain page, you can simply enter the page’s URL into the Pixel Debugger and the tool will let you know if it finds a valid Action, Engagement, or Sales tracking pixel on the page!

You can also see the most recent 500 pixels that have “fired” within the last 30 days which allows you to see exactly what is going on with your pages. 

So, if you think your pixels are firing, but they aren’t showing up in the list, then you know to check your pages to make sure that the pixels are in the ((body))...((/body)) section of your page.

And if the pixels are showing up in the list, but you’re not seeing the conversions in your tracking link, the Pixel Debugger will show you which tracking link is actually getting the conversion. Invaluable.

We definitely recommend that you become familiar using ClickMagick’s Pixel Debugger!
