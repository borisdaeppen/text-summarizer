Can I notify my client once all their clicks are delivered?

You sure can. And because we noticed that many click sellers were already adding the client’s email address to the “Notes” area when adding the client’s URL, we made it super easy to do.

If you want your client to receive an email update once all their clicks are delivered, all you have to do is include their email address surrounded by brackets in the “Notes” area, and that’s all there is to it.

So for example if your client’s email is mary@smith.com just add [mary@smith.com] anywhere in the “Notes” section for this client’s URL, and when their clicks are delivered they’ll receive the following email …
