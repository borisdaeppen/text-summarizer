How do I add a timer to a MagickPop or MagickBar?

Adding a timer to your MagickPops and MagickBars is easy.

First, create a timer, then insert your new timer into as many MagickPops or MagickBars as you’d like.

It’s simple using ClickMagick’s content editor!
