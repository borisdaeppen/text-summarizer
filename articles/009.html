Ok, so how should I best use the pop ups?

Your imagination is the only limit, and it really depends on what you’re promoting, but here are a few ideas ...
  • Offer a bribe or bonus if the user takes action
  • Promote other related products and services
  • Pop up an opt-in form to help build your list
You only have a few seconds to do it, but the best suggestion we can give you is to do SOMETHING to try to grab the user’s attention.

Try experimenting with audio or a video!
