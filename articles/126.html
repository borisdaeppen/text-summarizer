How do I view my Organic referring keywords?

To view keywords people used to find your pages in search engines, click the number in the TC column.

You’ll see all of your stats including clicks, conversions, EPC and more for each and every keyword.

Please note that Google rarely provides this type of keyword data any more, but occasionally they do, and you’ll also see all of your referring keywords from Bing, Yahoo and other popular search engines too.

Note: You can get all of your Google search keywords from the Google Search Console, which you can also sync with your Google Analytics account if you use GA as well.
