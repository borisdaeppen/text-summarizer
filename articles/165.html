Do you have Training Videos?

We sure do! We have an entire library of training videos.

You can find all of our training videos by looking in the Video Tutorials section of the Help menu.

You can also find video links on many of ClickMagick’s pages. Just look in the upper left-hand corner of the page to see if there’s a Help video for that specific page. The video link will normally look like this:
 
      Help

Most of our videos have a clickable Table Of Contents that you can reference at any time while the video is playing, so if you want to skip to a particular section of the video simply select the chapter you want to watch.

You can also watch our videos at double speed and get all the information in half the time!

Finally, if you can’t find a video on the topic that you’re interested in, feel free to submit a ticket to our 1-Hour Support Desk and make a request for it. While we can’ t shoot videos on demand, we can certainly add them to our project list for future possibilities.
