What are the Data Exporter & Importer used for?

The Data Exporter and Data Importer are found in the Tools menu at the top of any page. These tools import and export all of your link and rotator settings so you can reconstruct your setup in another account.

If you want to export your click data, simply click on the UC column of your link or rotator stats, then click on the Download CSV link in the upper right-hand corner:





Using these tools you can easily export data from your ClickMagick account for all sorts of purposes, including further analysis in other 3rd-party tools.

If you specifically want to export IP data, simply download your UC data and use a spreadsheet program to sort the results by IP address.
