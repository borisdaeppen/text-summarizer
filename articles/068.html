How do I make cool looking “transparent” MagickBars?

Easy! When you’re creating your MagickBar, simply select “Yes” for the transparent background option.

This will make the background disappear. For best results, make sure that any graphics you use in your MagickBar also have transparent backgrounds.

The possibilities are endless, but we’ve created a template in the editor to help you get started.
