What is the IP Manager used for?

The IP Manager is found in the Tools menu at the top of any page, and this is where you can manually enter IP addresses or IP ranges that you want to flag or block in your User Profile or in the Advanced “Bad Clicks” Settings of your tracking link or rotator.

For example, once you have tracking links set up you may want to flag your own IP address so that you don’t mess up your stats when you load or test your own pages, etc.

Or perhaps you’re getting a ton of fake or abusive clicks from an IP address that the ClickMagick system either hasn’t learned about yet, or one we can’t flag globally because it’s a dynamic residential IP address used by real people …

Just add the IP address in the IP Manager , then block your “User” clicks in your User Profile or in your individual tracking links and rotators and you won’t see clicks from that IP address any more.
