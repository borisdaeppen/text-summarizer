Can I show a favicon with my cloaked custom domain?

A favicon is a small icon that is associated with a website or web page that browsers display in different ways to web visitors, most commonly displaying it in or near the address bar.

Right now, there is no way to set a favicon with your cloaked custom domain. This is a feature we may add in the future if there is enough interest in it, but at the moment, there are no immediate plans to support favicons for cloaked custom domains.

If you have a strong need for favicons, please submit a ticket to our 1-Hour Support desk and let us know what you’re trying to accomplish so we can gauge the interest. Thanks!
