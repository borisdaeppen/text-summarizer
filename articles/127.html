How do I track conversions from Organic traffic?

Tracking conversions from your Organic traffic is super easy. In a nutshell you just need to add a conversion tracking pixel to your “thank you” pages – the same way you would to track conversions from tracking links.

Please note that your tracking pixels are account-wide and they will track all of your conversions for all of your tracking links AND your Organic traffic …

… so if you’ve already set up tracking pixels then you’re all set and you don’t need to do anything else.
