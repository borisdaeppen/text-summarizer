Where can I see a MagickPop in action?

ClickMagick pop ups can contain any type of content, and look like anything you want …

The best way to see this in action is to Create a MagickPop, assign it to one of your Links, then simply visit the link and you’ll see what a regular user would see.

You can also preview your pop ups at any time on the Links and MagickPops pages.
