How do I set up a custom domain with BlueHost?

BlueHost uses a very popular control panel called cPanel.

When you signed up for your BlueHost account, you should have received the login information for your cPanel account. If you have any trouble finding your account information, follow the instructions on this BlueHost helpdesk page:
 
  How to Log In to Your BlueHost Control Panel


Once you’ve logged into your cPanel account, follow the written step-by-step instructions in this FAQ to set up a custom domain with ClickMagick:
 
   How do I set up a custom domain with cPanel?
