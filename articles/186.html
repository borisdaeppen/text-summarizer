Set Up Your FourPercent Challenge Funnel


To get started, you’ll need to have your FourPercent Challenge Funnel Setup page open in a browser tab with the ClickMagick settings showing. It should look like this:





If you don’t already this page open in another browser window, you can open it now by CLICKING HERE. Be sure to click on the ClickMagick Setup tab to display your FourPercent Ref ID.
 
 
Tip: If you have any trouble getting to the FourPercent Setup page, it’s probably because you aren’t logged into your FourPercent account. Simply log into your account in a new browser window and CLICK HERE to try again.
 
 
Now that you have that page open in another browser window, you’re ready to set up your Challenge Funnel in ClickMagick.

Just click the button below to instantly set up your funnel. You will be asked for your ClickBank username and for your FourPercent Ref ID which you can get from your FourPercent Setup page. Once your funnel has been created, you will be given a Funnel ID number which you should write down. Click this button now to set up your funnel:


             
 
 
The final step is to copy your Funnel ID number to the ClickMagick Tracking Setup page. You should still have that open in another browser window. (CLICK HERE to reopen the page if necessary.) Enter your Funnel ID in the FourPercent Setup page and click Save Changes:



 
 
That’s it! Your FourPercent Challenge Funnel is all set up and ready to use!
