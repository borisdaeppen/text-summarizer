What is the Postback Builder used for?

The Postback Builder, which can be found in the Tools menu at the top of any page, is used to instantly generate customized Postback URLs for use with all of the popular affiliate networks.

Just select the affiliate network that you’re working with, click a button, and ClickMagick will provide you with a customized Postback URL that you can simply cut and paste into your affiliate network!

The Postback Builder will also help you add the “click ID” to your affiliate link so that postbacks will work correctly.
