Can I use ClickMagick with Pinterest?

You can, but you have to be careful. Pinterest does NOT allow “third party tracking redirects”, so that means you MUST use a cloaked custom domain for your tracking links that you want to use on Pinterest.

Make sure that both your custom domain and your individual tracking links are set to Cloaked, otherwise your links will redirect through our generic clkmg.com domain. This would be considered a “third party tracking redirect” and that’s exactly what Pinterest says they don’t allow.

Alternatively, you can also use any funnel entry links you’ve created for use with a FunnelMagick funnel, since these links points straight to your site and don’t redirect through ClickMagick at all.
