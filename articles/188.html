What is a Funnel page Variation URL?

With certain setups you may have multiple web pages that you want FunnelMagick to treat as a single page in your funnel – and Variation URLs are the way to do it.

For example, say you’re running paid ads on both Google and Facebook and you have a slightly different landing page for each – for compliance or conversion reasons – which is the start of your funnel.

To track this type of a setup just create the entry page of your funnel in FunnelMagick using one of the URLs, and then set up a Variation using the other URL and ClickMagick will do the rest.
 
 
Note: Funnel page variations have nothing at all to do with split testing – which is a completely separate functionality.
