How do I access my User Profile and User Settings?

You can access your user profile and account settings by clicking in the Account menu in the top-right of any page, and then selecting User Profile.

On your profile page you can set all sorts of options and settings for your account including your password, time zone, default date range for your stats, Backup URL, click auditing settings, traffic monitoring and alert settings, and a whole lot more.
