What does a yellow row in my stats mean?

If a row in your tracking link or rotator stats is highlighted in yellow, if means that you’re getting a higher than normal percentage of flagged clicks—more than 5% of the total.

A high percentage of flagged clicks doesn’t necessarily mean there’s a problem, but it’s worth looking into if you aren’t yet familiar with what flagged clicks are. This FAQ will fill you in:
 
   What are the 6 types of potentially “bad clicks”?

 
Note: On some displays, the yellow highlighting may look pink instead.
