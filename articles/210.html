Can I re-open my old account?

Unfortunately, it’s not possible to re-open a canceled account—all account data is deleted from our databases when an account is closed ...

You can, however, create a new account and get another 14-day free trial. If you want to use the same username and login information as before, simply contact us at our 1-Hour Support desk and we will release your previous username and email address.
