How can I easily build retargeting lists?

Retargeting is insanely powerful …

… the only problem is that you normally need to place a retargeting “pixel” on the site you’re promoting, which means you can only do it with your own pages.

Well not any more!

ClickMagick allows you to add a Facebook, Perfect Audience, AdRoll, or any other retargeting pixel to any tracking or rotator link—even when you’re promoting affiliate offers or sites you don’t own or control.

It’s super easy too.

Just go to the Advanced Settings for any link or rotator and look for the Pixel/Code section where you can insert your retargeting pixel, or other tracking code. It really is as simple as that.

For more details check out the video tutorial below which can be found on the Video Tutorials page in the Help menu:
 
Add a Retargeting Pixel Using a Tracking Link
 
 
Tip: You can even add retargeting pixels to sites such as Teespring, Facebook, YouTube, Google and other sites that can’t be “cloaked.” If you’re promoting a page on one of these sites and you add a pixel, ClickMagick will automatically redirect users through an invisible “page” containing your pixel.

Users won’t notice a thing and your retargeting pixel will be loaded, allowing you to build huge retargeting lists promoting pages on these hugely popular sites.
