Can I rename my ClickMagick affiliate account?

There are two different types of accounts that you can have with ClickMagick. The first is your user account where you actually use the ClickMagick service, and the other is your affiliate account which is used only for promoting the ClickMagick service to earn commissions.

To change your affiliate username, simply contact us at our 1-Hour Support desk and let us know what you want to change it to.

Your new affiliate username must be 4-20 characters, and as long as the name is available we’ll be more than happy to change it for you. Send us a couple of choices in case your first choice has already been taken.

Before changing your affiliate username, be sure to stop any traffic that’s directed at your current affiliate link or you may lose traffic that you’ve paid for.

 
Note: Keep in mind that your affiliate username is separate from the username for your regular ClickMagick account where you use ClickMagick. The affiliate username is only used with your

http://www.clickmagick.com/go/affiliate_username

link. Changing your affiliate username will not change the username for your regular account.
