How do I set up a cloaked custom domain?

With ClickMagick, it’s really simple to create a cloaked custom domain. Just follow the instructions in this FAQ, remembering to add the domain as a “cloaked” domain in ClickMagick’s Domain Manager:
 
  What is the recommended way to set up a custom domain?
 

If you need to uncloak an existing domain, follow these instructions:
 
   How do I cloak or uncloak an existing custom domain?
 
 
Tip: For best results, do NOT cloak (or mask) your domain with the domain registrar where you bought your domain. Many registrars do not do cloak their domains properly and it causes all sorts of non-obvious problems which is why their services remain uncorrected.

Leave your domain uncloaked with your domain registrar and let ClickMagick handle that instead.
